On this page you can choose:
1. United States: means sending funds to US Domestic Banks
2. International: sending outside United States Banks
Our tutorial is around Domestic Banks (US)

Step 4

Tick 'Other Electronic Transfers' and click on 'Next'

Step 5

Click on 'A Person', you also have the option to send funds to a company

Step 6

Type the First and Last Name of the Payee/Receiver/Drop

Step 7

Choose another bank and input the account number and routine number of the receiver/payee you're sending the funds to. Wire ABA Number is the same as Routine number of the Payee

Step 8

Fill in the info as I've done

Step 9

Review your Payee/Receiver info

Step 10

Tick 'Yes it's correct' and click Add

Step 11

Our Payee Bank has been added successfully. Click on 'Send Money Now'
Now let's enroll our bank in wire

Step 12

Click on 'Get Started' to enroll in Wire Transfer

Step 13

Accept to all their Terms and Conditions and Click on Next

Step 14

We've successfully enrolled in wire Transfer
Next thing is sending out money from the banklog you bought to drop/client you've added

Step 15

Enter the amount you would like to send to the added Payee/Receiver
Note: Each Wire Transfer fee is $25

Step 16

Wire is sent instantly and cannot be reversd so review throught for the last time to make sure informations are all correct, then click on Send

Step 17

Wire has been sent successfully
